About This Property

A custom-built masterpiece offering the perfect blend of sophisticated design and island comfort on Kiawah Island.

Exceptional Island Living

Welcome to 388 Governors Drive, an exquisite luxury vacation rental nestled in the heart of Kiawah Island. This stunning 4,025 sq ft home features a distinctive mahogany door with cut glass arch that welcomes you into breathtaking sight lines to the deck, pool, and serene lagoon.

The spacious living room showcases beautiful hardwood flooring, a gas fireplace, and tray ceiling with Anderson wood accent doors. The chef's kitchen is a culinary dream with granite countertops, ceramic beadboard backsplash, cherry and glass-front cabinetry, and a high-end Thermador gas range.

The sunroom is a year-round retreat with vaulted stained plank ceilings, skylights, and oversized windows offering stunning views. Relax by the private saltwater in-deck pool with lagoon vistas, or explore the beautiful natural lot with mature oaks, magnolias, cedar, and palm trees.
